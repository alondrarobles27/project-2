Enviromental Effects
Enviroment Juveniles Grow Up In
The enviroment these juveniles grow in has a great influence on their future life choices. Juveniles who grow up in so called bad or harsh neighborhoods have more of a risk into falling in drugs, joining gangs, or make bad life choices in general.
Enviroment Inside Prisions
Inside jails juveniles are more likely to experince violence, trasuma, sexual abuse, physical assault, or suicide. When inisde jails they don't have the same access to programs and services to further successfully re-enter their communities.
